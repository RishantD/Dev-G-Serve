Our Mission

Ever since social media has been established, it has been considered a very powerful advancement in society. It was designed to help you grow and prosper when used the right way. Sometimes we just need a teacher to show us the way to success and GooRoo plans to do just that. With GooRoo, we can make social media fun, useful, powerful, and beneficial just the way it was designed to be! We provide you with the perfect set of tools, ranging from networking with people to recruitment services. As an added bonus we also bring you a GooRoo’s take on what applications should be like. With us you will not only grow as a person but also become a guru for life!

Our Product

Here is why we love GooRoo and why you should too!

Dynamic Resumes

Never again be a number to a recruiter. GooRoo makes sure all of your applications and resumes help a recruiter get to know you as a person.

One Application

Looking for a job, seat in a college? No one enjoys filing so many different applications and that is why GooRoo has made it easy for you to fill one application and reuse information every time you start a new app. This way you spend more time on the parts that matter!

Mentorship

We are your social media GooRoo and we want to do our part in teaching you how to use social media right! We offer a free service allowing people to ask any questions they may have, about anything, and get it answered by one of our experts. We also introducing a concept where if you are learned enough to become a GooRoo we pay you to answer questions related to your field of expertise.

Intelligent Matching

When you GooRoo, you mean business. We make sure we match and suggest friends that have many similarities with your profile. Meeting friends, business partners, investors and employers just got simple

Search

No more of the boring scroll to find the information you need. The intelligent GooRoo makes all the information you may need readily available when you need it and where you need it

Recruiting

Looking for a good person for the job? We make it easy by looking for people that best match your job description and requirements. We not only bring you people that fit the position but also bring you the person with the right personality for the job.
